About

Written by "The Guy Behind the Lens"

Hi there! My name is Thomas Green and I am that guy. I recently moved from Savannah, GA up to the great city of Chattanooga, TN in the Spring of 2018. Over the last several years, I have also made Photography big a part of my life. Moving to Chattanooga I decided to start my own photography company. I wanted to create an experience that not only captured the moments that we all love and cherish, but also use my craft as a tool to to connect with people. Walking in Place, what a funny name. Life is fast and comes and goes like a flash of light, lets slow down and enjoy right now.
